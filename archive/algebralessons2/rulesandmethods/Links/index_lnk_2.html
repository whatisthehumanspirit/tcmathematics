Rules for Multiplying (and Dividing) Signed Numbers

1.  The product or quotient of two numbers with the same sign is positive.
2.  The product or quotient of two numbers with different signs is negative.
3.  Any product or quotient containing an even number of negative factors is positive.
4.  Any product or quotient containing an odd number of negative factors is negative.
