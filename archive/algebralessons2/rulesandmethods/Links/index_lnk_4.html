Method for Multiplying (and Dividing) Rational Numbers

1.  Change all division to multiplication by the reciprocal (Multiplicative Inverse)
2.  Reduce each rational in your product to lowest terms.
3.  Cross cancel common factors from numerators and denominators of factors.
4.  The product of the numerators over the product of the denominators is the product.
*assuming all possible reduction and cross canceling was done in step three, your product will be fully reduced but it never hurts to double check.
