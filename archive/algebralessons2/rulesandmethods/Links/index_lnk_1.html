Rules for Adding (and Subtracting) Signed Numbers

1.  Subtracting a number is the same as adding its opposite (Additive Inverse)
2.  If two numbers have the same sign, keep the sign and add their absolute values.
3.  If two numbers have different signs, keep the sign of the "larger" and subtract their absolute values, the "smaller" from the "larger".
